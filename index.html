Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up the SmartSlope?

SmartSlope arrives fully assembled and ready to use out of the box. Simply unfold it, adjust the slope to your desired angle, and you're ready to start practicing!

Can I use SmartSlope outdoors?

Yes! While SmartSlope is designed primarily for indoor use, it can be used outdoors on a flat surface. However, we recommend bringing it inside when not in use to protect it from the elements and extend its lifespan.

How do I adjust the slope?

The Elite model features digital slope control via the web control panel or smartphone app.

Does SmartSlope come with a warranty?

Yes! All models come with 1 year warranties. All warranties cover manufacturing defects and mechanical issues.

How do I clean and maintain my SmartSlope?

Regular maintenance is simple. Vacuum the surface occasionally to remove dust and debris. For deeper cleaning, use a mild soap solution with a soft brush, then rinse and let dry completely before storage. Avoid harsh chemicals that might damage the turf.
